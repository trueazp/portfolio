# Ethereum Market Trend & Volatility Analysis

## Context

Cryptocurrency markets are known for high volatility and rapid price fluctuations. Ethereum, as one of the largest cryptocurrencies by market capitalization, has experienced multiple growth cycles, market crashes, and high-risk periods over the last decade.

Understanding long-term trend behavior and volatility patterns is essential for data-driven decision making in high-risk financial environments.

---

## Problem Statement

How can historical Ethereum price data be analyzed to:

1. Identify long-term market trends  
2. Detect high-volatility periods  
3. Generate data-driven insights to support informed decision making
