# TheLook Ecommerce Business Intelligence Analysis

## Executive Summary

This project analyzes ecommerce business performance using customer behavior, product performance, operational efficiency, and revenue leakage analysis to support data-driven business decision making.

The analysis focuses on identifying growth opportunities, operational scalability, profitability optimization, and return-related revenue leakage within TheLook ecommerce platform.

---

## Key Results

- Identified strong long-term customer activity and revenue growth
- Detected acquisition concentration risk on Email traffic source
- Found profitability imbalance across product categories
- Evaluated fulfillment scalability during business growth
- Identified significant revenue leakage caused by product returns
- Developed strategic business recommendations for sustainable growth

## 1. Business Context

Ecommerce businesses face increasing challenges related to customer acquisition competition, operational scalability, profitability optimization, and product return behavior.

This project aims to evaluate how customer behavior, product performance, operational efficiency, and return activity impact overall business performance and revenue sustainability.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

How do customer acquisition patterns, product performance, fulfillment operations, and return behavior affect revenue leakage and overall ecommerce business performance?

Supporting analytical areas:

1. Customer acquisition & behavior analysis
2. Product and revenue performance analysis
3. Fulfillment & operational efficiency analysis
4. Return rate & revenue leakage analysis

## 6. Customer Acquisition & Behavior Analysis

<!-- INSERT VISUAL: Customer Acquisition Dashboard -->

The analysis evaluated:

- Traffic source contribution
- Customer activity trends
- Conversion performance
- Engagement distribution
- Behavioral activity patterns

### Key Insights

1. Email traffic contributed the largest share of customer activity.
2. Conversion performance across acquisition channels remained relatively homogeneous.
3. Customer activity demonstrated strong year-over-year growth.

### Business Interpretation

Customer engagement growth indicates strong platform expansion momentum. However, customer acquisition remains heavily concentrated on Email traffic despite similar conversion performance across alternative channels.

## 7. Product & Revenue Performance Analysis

<!-- INSERT VISUAL: Revenue & Product Performance Dashboard -->

The analysis focused on:

- Revenue contribution by category
- Profit margin comparison
- Revenue growth trend
- Category profitability distribution

### Key Insights

1. Revenue contribution was heavily concentrated within several major product categories.
2. High-revenue categories did not always generate the highest profitability.
3. Monthly revenue showed significant upward growth throughout 2023–2024.

### Business Interpretation

TheLook demonstrated strong revenue growth driven primarily by dominant categories such as Outerwear & Coats and Jeans. However, profitability analysis revealed the importance of balancing revenue growth with margin optimization.

## 8. Fulfillment & Operational Analysis

<!-- INSERT VISUAL: Operational & Fulfillment Dashboard -->

The analysis evaluated:

- Fulfillment duration trends
- Operational scalability
- Delivery performance consistency
- Operational stability over time

### Key Insights

1. Fulfillment duration remained relatively stable around three days.
2. Operational performance remained consistent despite business growth.
3. Increased customer activity did not significantly impact fulfillment efficiency.

### Business Interpretation

Operational processes demonstrated scalable performance capabilities. The business successfully maintained fulfillment consistency while customer activity and revenue continued to increase.

## 9. Return Rate & Revenue Leakage Analysis

<!-- INSERT VISUAL: Return Rate & Revenue Leakage Dashboard -->

The analysis focused on:

- Return rate trends
- Returned revenue analysis
- Leakage contribution by category
- Return behavior impact on profitability

### Key Insights

1. Return rates remained consistently high throughout the analysis period.
2. Revenue leakage was concentrated within high-volume product categories.
3. Categories with high transaction volume carried greater leakage exposure.

### Business Interpretation

Product returns represent a major profitability risk for TheLook. High return behavior directly impacts effective revenue retention and increases operational burden through reverse logistics activities.

## 10. Business Recommendations

### Customer Acquisition Strategy

- Diversify acquisition channels beyond Email dependency
- Improve retargeting and personalized marketing campaigns
- Strengthen omnichannel acquisition strategy

### Profitability-Driven Product Strategy

- Prioritize high-margin category growth
- Optimize category mix and pricing strategy
- Improve margin efficiency within high-revenue categories

### Operational Scalability Strategy

- Maintain fulfillment scalability during business growth
- Improve operational monitoring and forecasting
- Optimize fulfillment workflow and delivery coordination

### Return Prevention Strategy

- Improve product descriptions and sizing guidance
- Strengthen product experience quality control
- Develop return behavior monitoring systems
